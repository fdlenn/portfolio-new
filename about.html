About Me

Front-end developer based in the UK.

My name is Laura and I'm a front-end developer based in Folkestone, UK. I'm an Internal Communications Specialist by day and have been learning to code with SheCodes in my free time. I wanted to learn a new skill but also gain a better understanding of web-based design and structure. In this post-pandemic era, many organisations are opting to develop digital channels and apps as their primary communication tools, to engage with employees in remote workspaces.

I'm looking forward to learning more in the next stages of my course and seeing what opportunities arise from my new skills and knowledge. I'm keen to learn more about accessibility and how we ensure that people with all needs can navigate around sites and apps with ease.
